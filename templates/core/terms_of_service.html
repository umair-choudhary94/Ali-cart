User Guide

Greetings,Thank you for choosing AliCart. Below are step-by-step instructions on how to navigate our website.

Purchasing as a Guest:

You can make purchases without an account. During checkout, please specify your address and phone number. This information will allow us to contact you and discuss the details of your order.

Click the video below to visualize the process.

Creating an Account - Vendor:

If you wish to sell your products as well as buy, you'll need to create an account. Please click here to create an account. To sign up, enter your Gmail address and click 'Send OTP'. You will receive an OTP code; enter this code in the designated field below the email address to verify your identity. Once verified, you can set up your username and password to complete the registration and log in.
